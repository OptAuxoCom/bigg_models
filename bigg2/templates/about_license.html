About BiGG

BiGG is a knowledgebase of Biochemically, Genetically and Genomically structured genome-scale metabolic network reconstructions.

BiGG integrates more than 80 published genome-scale metabolic networks into a single database with a set of stardized identifiers called BiGG IDs. Gene in the BiGG models are mapped to NCBI genome annotations, and metabolites are linked to many external databases (KEGG, PubChem, and many more).

BiGG is hosted and maintained by the Systems Biology Research Group at the University of California, San Diego.
